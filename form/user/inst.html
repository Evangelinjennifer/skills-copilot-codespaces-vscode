Library Instructions

HOME

Finding Books

  1. Go to the library's website.
  2. Click on the library tab.

Checking Out Books

At the Checkout Desk

  1. Hand your library card and the book to the librarian.
  2. Wait for the librarian to scan the book and check it out to you.

Returning Books

Return Bin:

  1. Place the book in the designated return bin near the entrance.

Other Instructions

  1. Silence your phone and take calls outside.
  2. Return books and materials on time.
  3. Dispose of trash properly and keep the area tidy.

Asking for Help

  1. If you have questions, look for a librarian at the checkout desk or in the information area.
